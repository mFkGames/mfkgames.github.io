Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

At mFk Games (accessible from https://mfkgames.github.io/), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and players. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by mFk Games and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) directly on our website unless you choose to contact us. However, our mobile applications and games distributed through third-party platforms may utilize external services that collect information used to identify you.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

Our mobile games and apps integrate third-party libraries and SDKs to provide core functionalities, display advertisements, or analyze app performance. These third parties may access your non-personal data (such as device ID, advertising ID, and operating system version) according to their own privacy policies. Below are the links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers used by our applications:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Google Advertisement)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

3. Log Data and Crash Reports

We want to inform you that whenever you use our mobile games or applications, in case of an error or crash, data and information called Log Data is collected (usually through third-party tools like Firebase Crashlytics). This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our service, the time and date of your use, and other statistics to help us improve the game stability.

4. Children's Privacy

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet and mobile applications. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If a mobile application or game is designed specifically for children or families, we comply strictly with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) regulations and do not track personal or behavioral data. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website or within our games, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the official links provided on our main website.
