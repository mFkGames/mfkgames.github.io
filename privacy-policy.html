Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Welcome to mFk Games. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and share your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide, maintain, and improve our games and applications, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information Provided by You: Information you directly submit to us, such as your username, email address, or profile details when contacting support, creating an account, or participating in community features.
  • Device and Usage Data: When you access our applications, we automatically collect specific device information, including your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as IDFA or GAID), IP address, language settings, and crash logs/performance statistics.
  • Gameplay and Interaction Data: Details regarding your in-game activities, progress, achievements, feature utilization, and interaction times to help optimize gameplay balance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data strictly under lawful and legitimate grounds, primarily for the following purposes:

  • To operate, deliver, personalize, and improve our games and core application functionalities.
  • To detect, prevent, and debug technical issues, crashes, or potential fraudulent and unauthorized activities.
  • To provide technical support and respond directly to your customer service inquiries.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations, regulations, and law enforcement requests.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We value your trust and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. However, your data may be shared under the following restricted circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We partner with trusted third-party service providers (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, and cloud hosting infrastructure) that process data under strict confidentiality agreements solely to support app analytics and performance tracking.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or a government authority executing a valid legal request.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our web services and certain mobile interfaces may utilize cookies, pixels, or local storage mechanisms to retain your preferences, analyze traffic trends, and enhance the overall user experience. You can modify your device or browser settings to restrict or block cookies, though some features of the service may become unavailable as a result.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures (including SSL/TLS encryption during data transmission) designed to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Please note, however, that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Data Retention

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as GDPR, CCPA, or KVKK regulations), you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data. These rights include:

  • The right to request access to and a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to request the absolute deletion of your personal data from our systems ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • The right to withdraw any previously granted consent for data processing at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will become effective immediately upon posting the updated policy within the application. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
